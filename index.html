About Us

Welcome to The Khana Sutra

Khana simply means food in India. Sutra is a collection of aphorisms or proven truths in form of a manual that in ancient India were written down in books of palm leaves sewn together with a thread.

Shakespeare said, “If music be the food of love play on” And khanasutra adds, “If food be the music of life eat on”

So we bring together good food and good music
